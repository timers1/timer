How to play

2 people come up, 1 asker and 1 responder. The asker asks questions to try to get the responder to say yes/no. If the responder said yes or no before the time is up, the asker wins and takes the spot as responder and an asker comes up. If the responder didn't say yes or no within the 3 minutes, the responder wins and a new asker comes up. Underneath this is a timer for 3 minutes.
